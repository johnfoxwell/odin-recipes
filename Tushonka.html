Description

Canned stewed meat. Popular in Russia and other Eastern European Countries. Comman place name for other canned beef but not all pertain to GOST(govermental standard) Tushonka.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of Tushonka
  • Can opener
  • Spork
  • A good place to rest

Cooking Steps

  1. Use can opener to open Tushonka
  2. Devour delicious canned beef with goverment issue spork
  3. Relax by the fire with fellow comrades and enjoy campfire stories
