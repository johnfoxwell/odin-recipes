Description

National dish of Spain, tortilla-de-patatas, at its base is an egg and potatoe omelette cooked in oil. It can be served cold or hot and can optionally contain additional ingredients such as onion or other vegtables. A simple dish that anyone can make and can be served with any basically any occasion!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 medium Yukokn Gold potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow or white onion, sclied
  • 6 eggs, beaten
  • 2 tsp sea salt

Cooking Steps

  1. Add oil to large skilled over medium heat.
  2. Add potatoes and onion, season with 1 1/2 tsp salt, turn up heat to medium-high and cook for 8-12 minutes.
  3. Once potatoes are fork tender remove everything from the oil.
  4. In a seperate bowl beat 6 eggs and add 1/2 tsp salt. Once beaten add onion and potatoes.
  5. Add some oil to a 10-inch skillet over high heat and once hot add in egg mixture.
  6. Use rubber spatula to prevent edges from sticking and help the tortilla form its shape
  7. Once firm around the edges remove the pan from the heat and place a plate over the pan.
  8. Flip the pan with the plate on top and now with the cooked side facing up, place the tortilla back into the pan with the uncooked side facing down.
  9. Cook for an additional 2 minutes or until firm in the center.
  10. It is done when a toothpick is inserted into the center and it comes out clean.
