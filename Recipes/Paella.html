Description

A Spanish dish from the Valencia region, paella is named after the special cooking pan used to make the dish. A round, shallow pan made of carbon steel that heats quickly and evenly. A good paella pan will help the dish develop the socarrat, the crispy layer of rice crust that forms on the bottom of the pan. Also look for short grain-rice like Bomba or Valencia rice as long grain rice wont work since we are trying to get the rice creamy, long grain rice will take too long.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon grated lemon rind
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon saffron threads, crushed
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 8 (6-oz.) skin-on, bone-in chicken thighs, divided
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped yellow onion
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons chopped fresh thyme
  • 5 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 1/2 cups uncooked Bomba rice
  • 1/3 cup dry white wine
  • 3 cups unsalted chicken stock
  • 2 cups frozen green peas
  • 1 red bell pepper, torn into pieces
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley
  • Lemon wedges

Cooking Steps

  1. Stir together lemon rind, juice and saffron in a small bowl then set aside.
  2. Season all 8 chicken thighs with black pepper and 3/4 tsp salt.
  3. Heat oil in paella pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Add 4 chicken thighs to pan skin side down and cook for about 6 minutes.
  5. After 6 minutes turn chicken over and cook for another 3 minutes.
  6. Transfer cooked chicken to a plate and repeat steps with remaining chicken.
  7. Now add onion, thyme and garlic to pan and cook for about 4 minutes.
  8. Add rice and cook for 2 minutes, then add wine and cook for another 2 minutes.
  9. Stir in lemon mixture, stock and the remaining salt.
  10. Place chicken, skin side up, into dish. Cover and reduce heat to medium for 18 minutes.
  11. After 18 minutes uncover pan, add peas and red pepper pieces and increase heat to medium-high.
  12. Cook for 5 minutes or until rice begins to brown on the bottom/edge of pan.
  13. Remove form heat, sprinkle with parsley and serve with lemon wedges.
